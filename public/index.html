Put (red) in the text on your site

If like us you support (red) and everything they are doing at the moment to help people suffering from the effects of AIDS and HIV, you can help spread the word on your own website with one easy step.

Click the code to select it, then copy & paste it into your sites html above the </body> tag at the bottom of the page

Just copy the code into the HTML of your site (or blog), and we'll transform "red", wherever it may appear, into (red) and link it to them.
